About Us

Discover the story behind OmoCreations and our passion for stone fashion

OmoCreations is a premium fashion stone design company located at 3 Oyebo Street, Abiola Way, behind Laroy Suite, Abeokuta 111101, Ogun State, Nigeria.

We specialize in Sales of Beads, Rhinestones, Bridal Materials, and Paperstones. OmoCreations sells Original Rhinestones in Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria.

We also offer professional training on how to make paperstones in Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. Our mission is to bring elegance, beauty, and uniqueness to every fabric we touch.

With years of experience and a passion for perfection, we create masterpieces that stand out for weddings, parties, and special events.
